Gladiator Battle









-Punch: randomly chooses a number that is between your high and low damage

-Heal: if your rage is 10 or more, heal for +5

-Sacraficial Stare: sacrafice 25 health to gain +5 rage and hit your opponent for 30

-Mega Kick: if your rage is 50 or more, you are allowed to hit your opponent for 50

-Skip Turn: skip a turn to gain +30 rage

-Transform: if your rage is 100 or more, transform to a stronger gladiator by adding 25 to the high and low damage
